Simba

The Actual Wikipedia Page

Simba is a fictional character originally from the movie "Lion King", which is argualy one of the best Disney movies of all time. Simba goes from being an uncertain, nervous little cub to a kind and intelligent lion that is able to run the Pride Lands as efficiently as his father. He still maintains his mischevious and fun-loving side though.

Biography

Lion King

In the first Lion King, we see Simba's evolution from a young naive little cub to a wise and brave King. After the death of his father, Mufasa, Simba runs away and tires to escape his responsibilties. Eventually, however, he understands his duty and reclaims the throne.

Lion King 2

In Lion King 2, Simba is the attentive father, now trying to teach his daughter, Kiara, the rightful path, while maintaing his naughty side. He also grows more as a lion and learns to stop holding grudges.
